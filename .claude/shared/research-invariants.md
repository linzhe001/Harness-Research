# Research Invariants

## Purpose

These are domain-neutral process rules for projects using Harness Research.
They define how claims, protocols, documents, and lessons are allowed to form;
they do not define what any research field should believe.

## Invariants

- No evidence, no claim.
- Current docs must be grounded in current repo artifacts, logs, configs,
  metrics, or explicitly cited external sources.
- Dynamic research protocols are drafts until reviewed against evidence.
- Approved project contracts override operator preferences and protocol drafts.
- AI may propose stage transitions, contracts, and lesson promotion, but human
  approval is required for major workflow advancement and contract approval.
- Auto-iteration observations may produce findings, but may not directly
  promote content into `MEMORY.md` or research invariants.
- Stable architecture state and volatile experiment state must remain separate.
- Contract docs require stronger evidence than iteration notes.
- Stale docs must be archived before being replaced.

## Forbidden Content

Do not put domain-specific claims here, such as "metric X is required for all
projects" or "baseline Y is always mandatory." Those belong in current-project
evidence, protocol, or contract files after project-specific research.
