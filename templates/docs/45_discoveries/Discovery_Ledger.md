# Discovery Ledger

hypotheses, and lesson candidates that should inform the next experiment.
`MEMORY.md` remains reserved for accepted lessons only.

## Levels

- `observation`: run-level result, metric change, log event, or qualitative note.
- `phenomenon`: repeated or surprising pattern worth testing.
- `finding`: bounded comparison supported by artifacts.
- `hypothesis`: proposed explanation or next mechanism to test.
- `lesson_candidate`: possible lesson awaiting promotion review.

## Status Values

- `open`
- `testing`
- `supported`
- `weakened`
- `rejected`
- `promoted`

## Boundary Notes

Discovery entries are mutable and evidence-linked. They are not Approved
Contracts, Current Facts, or accepted lessons until promoted through the
appropriate review path.
