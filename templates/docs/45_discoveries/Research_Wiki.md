# Research Wiki

Status: active

This file stores stable searchable findings, method notes, open questions, and
cross-iteration summaries. It is context for later work, not Approval Evidence
or an Approved Contract.

## Entries

| ID | Type | Status | Summary | Evidence Refs |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Types

- `finding`: supported comparison or result summary.
- `method_note`: implementation or evaluation note useful for future runs.
- `open_question`: unresolved question that should inform the Experiment Queue.
- `paper_context`: stable writing context derived from Source Artifacts.

## Status Values

- `active`
- `superseded`
- `needs_review`

## Boundary Notes

Research Wiki entries must remain linked to artifacts, reports, metrics, or
run manifests. Promote only accepted lessons to `docs/50_memory/Lessons.md` or
`MEMORY.md`.
