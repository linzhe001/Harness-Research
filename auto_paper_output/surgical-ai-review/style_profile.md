# Style Profile

- Language: Chinese, academic technical review.
- Tone: cautious, citation-supported, trend-oriented.
- Do not write as a product pitch or blog manifesto.
- Use explicit claim boundaries: "not yet proven", "feasibility evidence", "component-level capability", "USER_GATE".
- Keep citation placeholders close to factual claims.
- Use English method names and citation keys unchanged.
- Figures should be titled in English per operator requirement, with Chinese caption drafts.
- Avoid declaring "democratization" as achieved; use it only as a potential direction.
