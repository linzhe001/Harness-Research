# Claims To Avoid

- Do not claim that surgical AI systems have already proven broad patient-outcome improvement.
- Do not claim that LC has already achieved a complete preoperative reconstruction to intraoperative 3D navigation loop.
- Do not claim that high-end surgical robots have no data or clinical value.
- Do not claim that standard laparoscopic video automatically solves privacy, labeling, outcome linkage, or real-world monitoring.
- Do not claim that NeRF, 3DGS, SLAM, or foundation models already provide a complete navigable surgical digital twin.
- Do not present paper titles and years mentioned by the PDF as verified bibliographic records.
