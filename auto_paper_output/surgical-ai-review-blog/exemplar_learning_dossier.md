# Exemplar Learning Dossier

## Source Style Signals

- The PDF moves between academic review planning and high-depth technical blog planning.
- The strongest later direction is a first-person-adjacent trend essay: make a route hypothesis, state why it is plausible, and explicitly mark evidence gaps.
- The useful narrative pattern is `why adoption is hard -> why data is the bottleneck -> why 2D video understanding is insufficient -> what a staged route could look like`.

## Reusable Moves

| move_id | observed_move | reuse_in_blog | boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| style_001 | Convert four parallel arguments into one route narrative. | Organize the blog as a single line from value to data to model to deployment. | Do not preserve every literature detail from the PDF. |
| style_002 | Define the route as a trend judgment. | Use wording such as `我更愿意把它看成一种值得下注的路线`. | Avoid claiming the future is settled. |
| style_003 | Separate LC video semantics from full 3D navigation. | Make LC the first flywheel and safety-assistance scene. | Do not overstate CT/MRI navigation relevance for ordinary LC. |
