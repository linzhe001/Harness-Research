# SOTA Gap Map

| area | what the PDF says is emerging | gap for blog framing | safe wording |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Adoption economics | Surgical robots and AI must cross clinical and economic value thresholds. | Cost-effectiveness varies by volume, platform sharing, learning curve, and long-term outcomes. | `先进技术必须回答价值医疗问题`. |
| Data infrastructure | Surgical AI data is narrow, center-biased, procedure-biased, and externally under-validated. | More video alone does not create a flywheel without labels, feedback, outcomes, privacy governance, and monitoring. | `数据入口必须扩大，但飞轮还需要反馈闭环`. |
| 3D representations | NeRF, 3DGS, SLAM, depth models, and foundation models are moving surgery beyond 2D perception. | Most methods still focus on visible surfaces or video semantics, not deep, deforming, navigable anatomy. | `从漂亮重建到可导航空间状态仍有距离`. |
| Route | Standard endoscopes can be a low-friction terminal; LC can be the first safety-assistance loop. | True preoperative-to-intraoperative 3D navigation may fit liver, kidney, pancreas, prostate, and complex pelvic surgery better than ordinary LC. | `LC 是入口，不是终点`. |
